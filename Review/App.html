NCLEX Review Mobile & Web App

🤝 Problem Statement

The NCLEX is a standardized exam that nurses must pass in order to become licensed and begin their careers in nursing. The exam is known to be challenging and can be a significant source of stress for new graduates. This app aims to solve the problem of inadequate preparation for the NCLEX, which can lead to lower pass rates and delayed entry into the workforce.

📈 Success Metrics

  • Modern, aesthetically pleasing mobile and web app
  • Easy to navigate access to practice questions, performance tracking, and learning materials.
  • Backend: a user-friendly content management system that allows for bulk csv/excel uploads of app content.

App Features

  • Practice questions and tests (timed and untimed)
  • Personalized learning tools: Flashcard templates and self-guided flashcards
  • Video tutorials
  • Ebook and study guide with bookmarking & highlighting
  • Calendar system with alerts
  • Performance Analytics and Tracking
  • Subscription Management

User Experience

  • Audience: Female 22-35; likely to be recent nursing school graduates who are looking for an effective way to prepare for the exam and pass on their first attempt. They are likely to be motivated, hard-working, and focused on achieving their goals. They may also be looking for a flexible and convenient way to study, such as on their mobile devices or through a web-based platform.
  • High-quality review content that covers all of the key concepts and topics that are tested on the NCLEX exam. The content is organized into easy-to-digest modules that allow users to focus on specific areas of nursing practice.
  • App provides detailed answer explanations for all practice questions and exams, helping users to understand why certain answers are correct and others are incorrect.
  • App will include a large bank of NCLEX-style practice questions and exams that simulate the format and difficulty level of the NCLEX exam. This provides users with a realistic preview of what to expect on test day and helps to reduce test anxiety.
  • Performance tracking and analytics: Provides users with detailed performance tracking and analytics tools that allow them to monitor their progress and identify areas where they need additional study time.

Backend

  • User-friendly content management system with ability to excel/csv bulk upload quiz questions, answers, detailed explanations, create a library of flashcard templates app users can select or create their own.
  • Ability to upload new logos, changing colors, banners, notifications, customize membership trials, and analyze user data.
